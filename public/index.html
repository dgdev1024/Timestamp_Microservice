Timestamp Microservice

Usage

In the address bar, at the end of the URL, pass in either an integer representing the UNIX timestamp of the date you want sent back, a string representing the natural form of the date you want, or "now" to get the current date timestamp.

Examples

When clicking into these links, notice the address bar above.

Natural Date: April 14, 2017
Unix Timestamp: 1492206365874
The Current Date: now
